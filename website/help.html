Most commonly asked Questions!

Question 1: How long does the delivery of the Ticket take?
Tickets usually get delivered to your Email inbox within 10 minutes of purchase. Make sure to also check your spamfolder.
Question 2: What if I want to have a printed version of my ticket?
The email sent by us will always include an attachment with a PDF of your ticket aswell as an invoice of your payment.
Question 3: How can I sell my eventticket on your website?
It is very simple, as everyone can in theory sell tickets for their event on our website via our ticketsell signup sheet. To prevent botting or scams all inquiries are manually checked to be legit upcoming events and are then verified and added for sale to our website.
